What Flat Earth Means To Modern Society

 

The idea for a flat earth rather than a spherical planet isn't a new one.  Most modern flat earth hypotheses stem for the English writer Sam Birley Rowbotham.  His 1800's conclusions about the Bedford Level experiment still today intrigue the reading public.  He went on to write several other expanded texts better explaining his ideas in the flat earth arena.

 

Today's modern flat earth societies, some based in passion others based on popularity, all share one common goal and that is finding out answers to their ever growing lists of flat earth questions.  This short list of book is a great place to start expand your own flat earth research.
